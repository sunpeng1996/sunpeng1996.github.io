Biography

Currently I am a Research Director at SenseTime. My goal is to find solution to real world problem using computer vision techniques. Currently my team works on developing algorithms for autonomous driving, scene understanding, mobile applications, remote sensing, etc.

I got my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering Department in the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2015. My supervisor is Prof. Jiaya Jia. Before that, I received the B. Eng degree from Computer Science and Technology Department in Zhejiang University in 2011, under the supervision of Prof. Zhihua Zhang.

I am recruiting self-motivated interns / full-time researchers in computer vision and deep learning. If you are interested in, please directly send your CV to my email.
